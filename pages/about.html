About Us

Chronos, a trusted name and brand leader in the watch
industry for over 100 years, is known the world over for its
uncompromising values: technical precision, innovative mindset,
quality craftsmanship and design excellence. The first to create
quartz crystal and titanium timepieces, Chronos was also an early
pioneer in advocating for the environment, launching the first
light-powered watches with proprietary Eco-Drive technology in 1976,
thus adding eco-mindful as a core value.
